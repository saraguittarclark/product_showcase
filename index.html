What We Do

We are an independent shipping vessel that is committed to providing flexible, reliable, and secure shipping solutions for our clients.

We specialize in refrigerated transport for produce, beverages, and medicines. We are certified in the handling and transfer of information, and are approved to carry passengers.

Where We Go

Our primary service area is between Earth and Saturn and the accompanying moons and colonies. Longer hauls or working outside of our service area may be subject to greater fees, but we are open to negotiation.
